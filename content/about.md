## The Linguist

My academic training in Slavic linguistics gave me something that proved essential throughout a career in national security: an analytical framework for how language actually works — not just what it says, but how it encodes meaning, fails across cultures, and shapes thought. A PhD is a long way of learning to ask better questions, and those questions have followed me everywhere.

## The Practitioner

Twenty-one years at the Department of Defense — most recently at the Defense Intelligence Senior Level — gave me a ground-level education in what linguistic failure actually costs. I worked at the intersection of language, intelligence, and AI/ML in environments where imprecision isn't an abstract problem.

## The Consultant

AC Stepan WordWorks is the synthesis of those two careers. I work with organizations that operate in high-risk, multilingual, or AI-dependent environments — where language decisions have legal, regulatory, or mission consequences.
