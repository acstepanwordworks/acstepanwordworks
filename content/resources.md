## Texas Czech Legacy Project

I volunteer with the Texas Czech Legacy Project at UT Austin, which uses AI-assisted transcription to preserve recordings of a dying Czech dialect spoken by immigrant communities in rural Texas — continuing a family legacy of language preservation that traces back to my great-grandfather Jan Štěpán, who emigrated from Bohemia in 1889.
